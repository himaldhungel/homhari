Our Story & Reach

Founded in 2018, HomHari is committed to agricultural excellence and farmer prosperity in Nepal.

Pioneering Agricultural Value Chains

HomHari, rooted in Chandrapur, Rautahat, is a premier crop aggregator. We are passionately dedicated to uplifting local farmers by providing a reliable bridge to Nepal's dynamic industrial sector.

Our mission is to cultivate sustainable and transparent agricultural value chains. We strive to empower producers with fair prices and consistent demand, while supplying industries with quality raw materials, thereby bolstering Nepal's food security and economic prosperity.
